Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a flavorful and versatile dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and seasonings. It’s a perfect way to use up leftover rice and can be customized with your favorite ingredients like chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

This simple recipe gives you a quick, tasty, and satisfying meal in under 20 minutes — perfect for a weekday lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set them aside.
  3. Add a bit more oil and sauté the vegetables until tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Mix in the soy sauce, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then garnish with spring onions before serving.

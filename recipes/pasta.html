Pasta

Description

This pasta recipe is simple and comforting, made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil. Perfect for a quick weekday meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and season.
